Our Beginning

Born from a Mother's Need for Gentleness

When our founder couldn't wear traditional perfumes during pregnancy, she and her husband crafted something revolutionary—solid perfumes that honor both sophistication and sensitivity. Each hand-poured creation delivers whisper-soft scents that embrace rather than overwhelm, proving that true luxury lies in thoughtful restraint.

Today, Sugre continues this legacy of gentle luxury, creating alcohol-free, travel-friendly solid perfumes that tell your story without overwhelming your senses or those around you.

The Art of Solid Perfume

Master the gentle application of your signature scent

Application Layering Ingredients Care & Storage

The Perfect Application

Warm the solid perfume between your fingertips for a few seconds. This gentle heat activates the natural waxes and releases the fragrance.

Apply to pulse points: wrists, behind ears, collarbones, and inner elbows. These warm areas help diffuse the scent naturally throughout the day.

Less is more with solid perfume. Start with a small amount and build as desired. The beauty lies in its subtlety.

✨

Create Your Signature

Solid perfumes are perfect for layering. Start with lighter, fresher scents and build towards deeper, warmer notes.

Apply different scents to different pulse points to create a unique fragrance journey that evolves throughout your day.

Our collection is designed to complement each other beautifully, allowing endless personalization.

🌸

Pure & Gentle

Our formula begins with natural waxes including beeswax and shea butter, creating a nourishing base that's kind to your skin.

Essential oils and premium fragrance compounds are carefully balanced to deliver sophisticated scents without overwhelming.

Free from alcohol, parabens, and harsh chemicals. Every ingredient is chosen for both its aromatic beauty and skin-loving properties.

🌿

Preserve Your Perfume

Store your solid perfume in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Heat can soften the balm and affect the fragrance.

Keep the tin closed when not in use to preserve the scent's integrity and prevent contamination.

With proper care, your solid perfume will maintain its quality for 12-18 months, though we suspect you'll use it long before then.
